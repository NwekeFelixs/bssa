history_edu Our Story

More Than a Club.
A Community.

Founded in 2009, Buckeye Blazers Soccer Academy (BBSA) was born from a simple realization: local talent deserved elite training without the "premier club" price tag. As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, we reinvest every dollar back into our players, facilities, and coaching education.
